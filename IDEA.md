Apersonal finance tracker focused on visualizing income streams and tax liabilities for freelancers and gig workers.

- Aggregate earnings from multiple platforms (Upwork, Stripe, PayPal, direct deposit) via API and CSV import.
- Automatically estimate quarterly tax withholdings per jurisdiction based on real-time income data.
- Provide a "Runway Calculator" projecting cash flow gaps between contract payments.
- Generate one-click, accountant-ready profit/loss statements and 1099 summaries.
